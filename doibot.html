The Wikipedia citation bot

by Smith609, design adapted from Luxo's

Tidies Wikipedia citations, and adds missing data and DOIs

Welcome to the Citation bot.

Enter the name of a page to fill in missing DOIs, and to complete other empty fields in {{cite journal}} templates.


Thorough mode - a slower, but more exhaustive, search. Finds PMID parameters and flags "broken" DOIs.
Fast mode - cuts a few corners, so may miss a few DOIs.
Turbo mode. Only checks the CrossRef database.
Commit edits. Leave blank to check Wikicode.

Please enter your Wikipedia username below before submitting your query.
